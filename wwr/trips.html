Explore Our Rafting Trips

Our Half-Day Thrill

Our Half-Day Thrill is the perfect introduction to whitewater rafting! We take you on a 3-hour guided adventure through gentle but exciting rapids — ideal for families, first-timers, or anyone looking for a quick escape into nature. Let us help you create unforgettable memories on the river!

Full-Day Adventure

Our Full-Day Adventure is a favorite among groups and outdoor enthusiasts. We guide you along a thrilling river route filled with moderate rapids, peaceful stretches, and stunning scenery. We even provide a delicious riverside lunch so you can relax and recharge while surrounded by nature.

Extreme Rapids Expedition

For the ultimate adrenaline rush, join our Extreme Rapids Expedition. We’ll take you through fast, technical, and wild Class IV+ rapids with professional guides who prioritize your safety and thrill. This is our most intense trip — perfect for seasoned rafters and bold adventurers!
